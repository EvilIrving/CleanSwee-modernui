# Product

## Register

product

## Users

CleanSweep is for Android users who want to sort large personal photo and video libraries locally, often in short sessions on a phone. They care about privacy, storage, speed, and clear recovery from mistakes.

## Product Purpose

CleanSweep helps users reclaim storage and organize media through direct gestures, folder actions, and duplicate scanning without sending data off device. Success means users always know what state they are in, what action will happen next, and how to undo or review pending changes.

## Brand Personality

Private, practical, calm. The interface should feel efficient and trustworthy rather than playful for its own sake.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative dashboards, vague card grids, overloaded dialogs, ambiguous destructive actions, and low-contrast Android defaults that make each step feel the same.

## Design Principles

Make state visible before asking for action.

Keep primary actions close to the object they affect.

Prefer inline feedback, progress surfaces, and reversible review over blocking dialogs.

Use one restrained component vocabulary across setup, sorting, duplicate review, and settings.

Treat language as part of discoverability, not a secondary preference.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Keep controls touch-friendly, readable in light and dark themes, and usable through system locale or explicit app language selection. Avoid relying on color alone for selection, warning, or progress states.
